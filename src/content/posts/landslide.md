> [!QUOTE] *But time makes you bolder* 
> *Even children get older* 
> *And I'm gettin' older, too*
>
> — Landslide by Fleetwood Mac

I walk outside on a cold winter morning. The chilling wind hits like needles on my cheeks. 

Notes:

Themes of passage of time
Themes of finding strength in hard times (perseverence)\
How the song continues to resonate with me more and more over time. 
How the song make me teary everytime I listen to it.
How you can get caught up over the dumbest things when caughtup in the whirlwind of life
Feeling present

cold winter morning, wind stinging my cheeks, barely able to open my eyes, tired, and dreading. Every sensation so unpleasant, but also so alive.
